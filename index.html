Our purposes

The values we pursue

Cooperation
Cooperation in our club is one of the main values. Since we often conduct group work at meetings, the activity of each member is extremely important for us to create a warm and reliable atmosphere in the club.
Culture
Our club strives to understand the culture of Japan through the anime. Anime is one of the most important component of the spiritual life of Japanese society and has a large army of fans in all countries.
Ethics
Ethics in the anime club is one of the most important components, being an aspect of the Japanese culture we study. Through the prism of our lives, we will examine foreign norms of behavior through the study of anime.
We believe that our club will fulfill the dreams of the newcomers, whatever they may be. With our help, you can develop your uniqueness and personality. We watch animation together, learning more about Japanese culture and developing our sociality, striving to combine viewing with benefits.
